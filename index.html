In 2010, a Shiba Inu puppy named Fury decided to pursue his dream of becoming the world's best dog. He traveled all over the world to learn everything about being man's best friend. After three years abroad, Fury finally realized his dream and he became the world's best and most loved dog. He eventually returned home to Milwaukee and opened his very own obedience training school, named Shiba Inu Dojo, so he could pass on his knowledge to the future generations.

The Shiba Inu Dojo employs a rigorous, but holistic cirriculum that enhances and brings out the best qualities of its students. Dogs of all breeds are welcome at the Shiba Inu Dojo and whether you are a puppy or adult, with our guidance, every dog will thrive at our school. The dojo has always had a 100% graduation rate since its first cohort because no dog is ever left behind. Graduates of the dojo go on to be successful dogs in society and lead rewarding lives.

Courses are 12 weeks long and we offer 3 terms every year, housing included. Please contact us to learn more or if you have any questions about enrollment.

Pricing per course is 5,000 Dogecoins. Scholarships are available for students based on financial need. Please contact us to inquire about scholarship opportunities.
